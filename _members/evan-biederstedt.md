Evan is a computational biologist with a background in cancer genomics. He is now spearheading the development of the Cell Annotation Platform (CAP), infrastructure designed to improve our ability to interpret cellular state by facilitating the accumulation, sharing and analysis of relevant metadata and molecular signatures. His GitHub profile can be found [here](https://github.com/evanbiederstedt).
