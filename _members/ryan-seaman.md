Ryan is an MMSc student in the Biomedical Informatics program at Harvard
Medical School and a member of the HIDIVE Lab.

He received his B.A. in Computational Biology from Colby College. Prior
to graduate school, he worked as a Bioinformatician at MDI Biological
Laboratory, studying regeneration and aging.

Ryan's thesis project, VitessceGen, is a multi-agent LLM system that
automatically generates validated Vitessce visualization configurations
from natural language input and biomedical data.
